The Fanlens Suite

Tagger

We took the hassle out of AI! Add any Facebook page you want and immediately get your own personalized machine learning model.

You can start with some smart suggestions provided by us and simply add your own. Each click trains your little pet robot to become smarter and smarter!

What can you do with it?

  • Smart filtering of comments and postings!
  • Gain insights about the mood in your community!
  • Detect fraud and scams!
  • And much more! There is no limit, add any tags you want and let the Tagger do the work for you!

Evaluator

No more uncertainty before you post! Fanlens isn't limited to analyzing your fan base, your Tagger models can be used to evaluate your own postings as well.

Enter your post, press Evaluate and immediately know how your post will be received. If your happy with it you can post it right away or batch it up and delay it until a better time.
